CrashiQ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. General Provisions

SBG Technologies Inc. (hereinafter referred to as "Company") values the personal information of users who use the CrashiQ service (hereinafter referred to as "Service") and complies with relevant laws and regulations including the Personal Information Protection Act.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information the Company collects, how it is used, and what measures are taken to protect it.

2. Personal Information Collected

2.1 Information Collected During Membership Registration

  • Required: Name, Email address, Phone number, Password
  • Optional: Company name, Date of birth, Gender

2.2 Information Collected During Service Use

  • Vehicle Information: Vehicle number, Model, Year, VIN, Mileage
  • Accident Information: Accident location, Time, Damage photos, Accident description
  • Location Information: GPS coordinates (only when using the service)
  • Service Usage Records: Access logs, IP address, Device information

2.3 Information Collected Through Social Login

  • When using social login (Kakao, Naver, Google, Apple), basic profile information is collected with user consent

4. Personal Information Retention and Use Period

Principle: Personal information is destroyed without delay after achieving the purpose of collection and use.

4.1 Retention Period by Information Type

  • Member information: Until membership withdrawal
  • Service usage records: 1 year
  • Accident processing records: 3 years

4.2 Retention According to Laws and Regulations

7.1 User Rights

  • Right to correction and deletion

  • Right to withdraw consent

7.2 Exercise Methods

8. Personal Information Protection Measures

8.1 Technical Measures

  • Encryption of personal information (AES-256)
  • SSL/TLS encrypted communication
  • Firewall and intrusion detection system
  • Regular security updates
  • Access log retention and monitoring

8.2 Administrative Measures

  • Minimum access rights principle
  • Regular employee security training
  • Personal information protection pledge
  • Regular security audits

8.3 Physical Measures

  • Access control to data centers
  • Document security and shredding
  • CCTV monitoring

9. Use of Cookies

The Company uses cookies to provide personalized service and improve user experience.

9.1 Purpose of Cookie Use

  • Login status maintenance
  • User preference storage
  • Service usage analysis
  • Customized content provision

9.2 Cookie Management

Users can refuse cookie installation or delete stored cookies through browser settings.

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

10. Children's Personal Information Protection

1. In principle, the Service does not collect personal information from children under 14.

2. Users must be at least 14 years old to register for the Service.

3. If personal information of children under 14 is inadvertently collected, it will be destroyed immediately upon discovery.

11. International Data Transfer

Personal information may be stored and processed on servers located overseas for service provision.

  • Transfer destination: United States (AWS), Singapore (GCP)
  • Transfer items: All collected personal information
  • Transfer method: Encrypted transmission
  • Security measures: Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) application

12. Privacy Policy Changes

1. This Privacy Policy may be amended according to changes in laws or service policies.

2. When making significant changes, we will notify users at least 7 days in advance.

3. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes agreement to the amended Privacy Policy.
