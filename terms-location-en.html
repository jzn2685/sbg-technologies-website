CrashiQ Location Information Terms of Use

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Article 1. Purpose

These Terms govern the collection and use of location information in the CrashiQ service (hereinafter "Service") provided by SBG Technologies Inc. (hereinafter "Company").

Article 2. Location Information Collected

The Company collects the following location information:

  • Accident occurrence location (GPS coordinates)
  • Vehicle location at the time of accident
  • Driving route information
  • Location of service providers (repair shops, insurance companies)

Article 3. Purpose of Location Information Use

Collected location information is used only for the following purposes:

  1. Accurate accident location identification and emergency assistance
  2. Nearest repair shop and towing service guidance
  3. Insurance company field dispatch service connection
  4. Accident analysis and statistical data generation
  5. Service quality improvement and new service development

Article 4. Location Information Retention Period

1. Location information is destroyed immediately after achieving the collection purpose.

2. However, it may be retained for the specified period in the following cases:

  • Insurance processing: 3 years
  • Legal retention obligations: Period according to relevant laws
  • Dispute resolution: Until dispute resolution

Article 5. Third-Party Provision of Location Information

1. The Company does not provide location information to third parties without user consent.

2. It may be provided in the following cases:

  • When users have given prior consent
  • When requested by emergency rescue agencies
  • When required by law

Article 6. Location Information Protection

The Company takes the following measures to protect location information:

  • Encrypted storage of location information
  • Minimization of access rights
  • Security system establishment and operation
  • Regular security audits

Article 7. User Rights

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  1. Withdrawal of consent to location information collection
  2. Request to stop using location information
  3. Request to confirm location information collection history
  4. Request to delete location information

Article 8. Changes to Terms

1. The Company may amend these Terms as necessary and will notify users through the Service.

2. Amended Terms take effect from the time of notification.
