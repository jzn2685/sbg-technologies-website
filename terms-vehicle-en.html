CrashiQ Vehicle Information Collection and Use Agreement

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Vehicle Information Collected

CrashiQ Service collects the following vehicle information:

  • Basic Vehicle Information
    • License plate number
    • Make and model
    • Year of manufacture
    • Vehicle color
    • Vehicle Identification Number (VIN)
  • Vehicle Condition Information
    • Mileage
    • Damage location and severity
    • Accident history
    • Maintenance history
  • Vehicle Photo Information
    • Pre/post-accident vehicle photos
    • Detailed damage photos
    • Vehicle registration document photos

2. Vehicle Information Collection Methods

  • Information directly entered by users
  • AI automatic recognition through vehicle photos
  • Information collection through insurance company integration
  • Information collection through repair shop integration

3. Purpose of Vehicle Information Use

  1. Accident Processing Support
    • AI-based damage analysis and repair cost prediction
    • Appropriate repair shop matching
    • Insurance processing support
  2. Service Provision
    • Customized maintenance recommendations
    • Vehicle management notification service
    • Accident prevention information provision
  3. Statistics and Analysis
    • Accident pattern analysis
    • Service quality improvement
    • Used only in forms that cannot identify individuals

4. Vehicle Information Retention Period

Principle: Destroyed without delay after achieving collection purpose

Exceptional Retention:

  • Insurance claims: 3 years
  • Tax-related materials: 5 years
  • Consumer disputes: 3 years
  • Legal retention obligations: Period according to relevant laws

5. Third-Party Provision of Vehicle Information

Principle: Prohibition of third-party provision without user consent

Cases of Provision:

Recipient Items Provided Purpose
Insurance Companies Vehicle info, Accident info Insurance processing
Repair Shops Vehicle info, Damage info Repair estimate and maintenance
Emergency Services License plate, Location info Emergency assistance

6. Vehicle Information Protection Measures

  • Encrypted storage and transmission of vehicle information
  • Access rights management and minimization
  • Regular security audits
  • Automatic masking of personal information in vehicle photos
  • Security incident response system establishment

7. User Rights

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Request to access vehicle information
  • Request to correct or delete vehicle information
  • Request to stop processing vehicle information
  • Request to transfer vehicle information (data portability)

How to Exercise Rights: Through the app settings menu or customer service center (+82-1588-0000)

8. Withdrawal of Consent

Users may withdraw consent for vehicle information collection and use at any time.

However, CrashiQ service use may be restricted upon withdrawal of consent.
